TREK SUMMARY

Ghorepani Poonhill trek is one of the most beautiful short trekking in Nepal offering spectacular view of Dhaulagiri and Annapurna Range of Himalayas. Ghorepani Poon Hill Trekking is mostly famous for breathtaking sunrise view on Himalayas. In ancient time, this route was trade route. The name Ghorepani was derived from Ghoda (which means Horse in Nepali language) and Pani (water in Nepal) means a place to fetch water for horses and take good rest. The main peaks that can be seen from Poonhill are Annapurna South, Annapurna I, Annapurna II, Annapurna III, Annapurna IV; Hiunchuli, Machhaphuchre, Dhaulagiri I,II,III,IV, V, Lamjung Himal, Gangapurna and other peaks. The maximum altitude of Ghorepani Poonhill trek is 3210m at Poonhill, which makes Ghorepani Poon Hill trek accessible for any age group. The Trekking trail for Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek is through the upstairs for 1st day mainly from Hile to Ulleri ( If you would like to avoid stairs then we can take Jeep upto Ulleri), and then through the midst of forest, mostly uphill and few flat surface and downhill. We can see waterfalls and small rivers during the Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek. If you have more number of days available to spend in Nepal we can add Annapurna Base Camp trek with Ghorepani Poon Hill trek which will take 10 trekking days.

FAQ's

Generally Ghorepani Poon hill Trek is of 4 days trek making small trek circuit, if you are limited with trekking days in Nepal then we can do Ghorepani Poon hill Trek in 2 days (using same trekking path not making circuit), or in 3 days (Covering more distance by Jeep drive and trekking more per day).

Jogging, some physical exercises, stretching can be good to do Ghorepani Poon hill Trek as you have to walk nearly 6 hrs per day in the stone paved staircase, through the forest, uphills and downhills. Prior exercise can be beneficial for trekkings in Nepal.
